How to Use
The quickest and easiest way to use GptGO is Quick Search:
  • Highlight any section of text with your cursor
  • Right click and select "Search text and get response as notification"
  • Within a few seconds, you should receive a notification with ChatGPT's response
Give it a try on the prompt below:
How are you doing today, ChatGPT?
To read longer responses and search custom prompts, use the GptGO popup:
  • Highlight any section of text with your cursor
  • Click on the puzzle piece from the top right of your browser
  • Select GptGO from the list, the popup should open
  • If needed, edit your prompt
  • Click the search button or hit ENTER
Give it a try on the prompt below:
Write a short essay on the benefits of AI
Troubleshooting FAQ
Why am I not getting a notification from Quick Search?
+
  • Make sure notifications are enabled on your computer
  • Make sure notifications are enabled on your Google Chrome account
  • Make sure your computer is not on Do Not Disturb
Why doesn't my highlighted text show up in the popup?
+
  • Refresh the pages that were open prior to GptGO being installed
  • Due to the nature of some forms of text, GptGO is unable to retrieve the text you have highlighted. In this case, please copy and paste your desired prompt into the popup
Why do I keep get errors when I send search requests?
+
  • ChatGPT may be down, or you may have used up your free grant given by OpenAI
  • To check your usage, visit your account usage page
  • If you have used up your grant, visit the API Usage section to learn more about how and why you should switch to a "pay as you go" subscription
API Usage
GptGO utilizes OpenAI's Text-Davinci-003 API, which grants all new users 3 months or $18 of free API usage. After that, users will need to upgrade to a "pay as you go" subscription. You can check your current API usage on your account usage page

To clarify, GptGO will always be free but users must pay OpenAI to continue making search requests to ChatGPT after their free usage grant has expired.

Benefits of a "pay as you go" subscription include:
  • Accessibility:  use ChatGPT even when the website is overloaded with traffic!
  • Convenience: never open a new tab or log in to use ChatGPT again!
  • Affordability: only pay for what you need, most users only pay $3 to $5 per month!

Ready to commit? Follow the steps below to switch to a paid subscription:
  • Go to your OpenAI account billing page and log in
  • Click on "Set up paid account" and complete the payment form
  • Congrats! You now have a paid subscription and can use GptGO forever
