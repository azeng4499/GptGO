How to Use
The quickest and easiest way to use GptGO is Quick Search:
  • 1 ) Highlight any section of text with your cursor
  • 2 ) Right click and select "Search text and get response as notification"
  • 3 ) Within a few seconds, you should receive a notification with ChatGPT's response
Give it a try on the prompt below:
How are you doing today, ChatGPT?
To read longer responses and search custom prompts, use the GptGO popup:
  • 1 ) Highlight any section of text with your cursor
  • 2 ) Click on the puzzle piece from the top right of your browser
  • 3 ) Select GptGO from the list, the popup should open
  • 4 ) If needed, edit your prompt
  • 5 ) Click the search button
Give it a try on the prompt below:
Write a short essay on the benefits of AI
Troubleshooting FAQ
Why am I not getting a notification from Quick Search?
  • 1 ) Make sure notifications are enabled on your computer
  • 2 ) Make sure notifications are enabled on your Google Chrome account
  • 3 ) Make sure your computer is not on Do Not Disturb
Why doesn't my highlighted text show up in the popup?
  • 1 ) Refresh the pages that were open prior to GptGO being installed
  • 2) Due to the nature of some forms of text, GptGO is unable to retrieve the text you have highlighted. In this case, please copy and paste your desired prompt into the popup
