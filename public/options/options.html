How to Use
  • 1 ) Highlight any section of text with your cursor
  • 2 ) Open the GptGO chrome extension from the top right part of your browser
  • 3 ) The text you highlighted will already be in the prompt, and you can edit your prompt if needed
  • 4 ) Click the button or simply use CMD + SHIFT to search
  • **First time users may need to restart browser**
    • Give it a try on the sentence below:
    How are you doing today, ChatGPT?
